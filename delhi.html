  1. All about IIT Delhi Campus

    2. The campus of IIT Delhi was established in 1961 to develop the science, engineering, and technology field. It is situated in Hauz Khas in South Delhi. IIT Delhi Campus Area is divided into four functional zones. Residential Zone for students that includes hostels, students’ canteen etc. Faculty and Staff Residential Zone Recreational Area for students that includes the football stadium, lawn tennis courts and swimming pool, cricket ground, basketball courts, Student Activity Center (SAC), hockey field. Academic-Zone that includes department offices, lecture theatres, libraries, and workshops

    Since its inception, over 40096 students have graduated in various fields in which 4101 have received Ph.D., nearly 13326 have B.Tech. Degree and rest of the students have completed their master’s degree from IIT Delhi. To be eligible for graduate and postgraduate in IIT Delhi, students need to qualify some entrance exams such as JEE, JAM, GATE, CAT, CEED, etc. These Entrance Exams offer various courses in IIT Delhi

  2. IIT Delhi Fests/Events

    3. As you all know IIT Delhi Campus is most famous for its infrastructure with gardens, lawns, residential complex, and wide roads. It organizes many events in which Tryst and Rendezvous is the largest festival in India. The annual technical festival Tryst is organized by Co-curricular and Academic Interaction Council (CAIC) and Rendezvous is organized by Board for Recreational and Creative Activities (BRCA)

    Tryst (Festival) – It is an annual technical festival organized by IIT Delhi students in February and March. Tryst IIT Delhi includes the events of: -

    • International Robotics Challenge
    • International Research Competitions, HACK-IT
    • National Junior Robotics Challenge
    • Android Hackathon
    • Quadcopter Challenge
    • IOT Challenge
    • Asgard

    Rendezvous – It is the largest cultural festival in north India conducted by IIT Delhi. It is a four-day-long event held in October. Rendezvous IIT Delhi organizes 100+ competitive events with 20 lakh prize money

  3. Placements and Internships Offered at IIT Delhi

    4. IIT Delhi is considered the best institute in all over India. The reason is highly placement records. Every year, more than 400 companies come to the campus and 900+ students get placed with high packages. In 2019, The top recruiting companies are: -

    • Intel
    • Goldman Sachs
    • Samsung Research
    • EXL
    • Microsoft

    IIT Delhi provides two internships for design units. The first one is the Summer Internship for 50 working days after the 6th semester. The main motive of the IIT Delhi Summer Internship is to provide the practical knowledge of theoretical concepts. This internship helps the students in their final year projects. And the second one is a Semester-long internship of 100 days duration, in which a student can earn 5 design units.
