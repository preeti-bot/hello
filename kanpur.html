IIT KANPUR

IIK, one of the oldest and most sought-after engineering institution located in Kanpur,Uttar Pradesh.It was declared to be an institute of national importance by the Government of India under the institute of Technology Act. Established in 1959 as one of the first Indian Institutes of Technology, the institute was created with the assistance of a consortium of nine of US research universities as part of the kanpur Indo-American Programme(KIAP). Being a residential campus and sprawled over 420 hectares of land,IIT K offers accommodation to over 1000 faculty and staff members and around 4000 students. The institute has three primary schools for children in its campus and a central school for students up to class 12. A great emphasis is laid on the research and development works and for that , superb facilities have also been made available for the scholars. IIT Kanpur was establishied by an Act of Parliament in1959. The institute was started in december 1959 in the room in the canteen building of the Harcourt Butler Technological Institue at agriculture gardens in Kanpur. In 1963,the institute moved to its present location, on the grand truck road near the village if kalyanpur in kanpur district.The institute has around 6478 students with 3938 undergraduates and 2540 postgraduates and about 500 research associates.

  2. National Events at IIT Kanpur
    • Antaragni

      • Antargni is a non-profit organisation run by the students of IIT Kanpur.It was founded entirely by the students Gymkhana club of the university when it began. Today the budget is around 1 crore which has been raised through sponsorship. It began as an inter-collegiate cultural event in1964 and now draws in over 30,000 visitors from 300 colleges in India Annual cultural festival held over 4 days in october. The festival includes music,drama,literary games and quizzes.It has 100,00+ liked fan page on facebook. There os a YouTube channel didicated to festival with 1,000+ subscribers.

    • Techkriti

      • Techkriti was started in1995 with an aim to encourage interest and innovation in technology among students and academia to interact. Megabucks, a business and entrepreneurship festival which was earlier held independently merged with Techkriti in the year,2010. Notable speakers at Techkriti have included APJ Abdul Kalam, Vladimar voevodsky,Douglas Osheroff,Oliver smithies,Rakesh Sharma,David Griffiths and Richard Stallman. It has a 50,000+ liked fan page on Facebook and Youtube channel dedicated to the festival has 250+ subscribers.

    • Udghosh

      • Udghosh is an annual inter-college sports meet held in the month of September every year. Udghosh involves students from all over India competing in the university’s sports facilities. The festival includes Motivational Talks, Mini Marathon, Gymnastic Shows and Sports Quizzes to various sports events.

    • Vivekananda Youth Leadership Convention

      • Vivekananda Samiti, under Students Gymkhana, on behalf of IIT K, has undertaken the celebration of 150th Birth Anniversary of Swami Vivekananda from 2011-2015. The convention has included Kiran Bedi, Bana Singh, Yogendra Singh Yadav, Raju Narayana Swamy, Arunima Sinha, Rajendra Singh and other personalities from different fields in previous years.

    • E-summit

      • The E-Summit at IIT K started in the year, 2013. The first E-Summit was scheduled for 16-18 Aug 2013. The three day festival by Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kanpur on the theme Emerge on the Radar included talks by eminent personalities, workshops, and competitions.
